Ripple Effect

What we do.

Prevent the spread of HIV/AIDS by raising awareness and encouraging activism through knowledge, creativity and service.

How we do.

1. Become advocates for the stopping of the spread of HIV/AIDS by becoming knowledgeable about the implications of the disease and partnering with relevant campus departments.
2. Be a pillar in the Atlanta community by completing community service projects across Atlanta aiding those suffering from HIV/AIDS.
